=== Clean restore

Ransomware Resilience now supports clean restores. With clean restores, Ransomware Resilience isolates infected workloads to identify encryption and remove malware; it also recommends recovery options to increase the recovery time and reduce the amount of data loss. Clean restores provide a guided, step-by-step restoration process to deliver a clean, malware-free workload restoration that prevents reinfection and minimizes data loss.

For more information, see link:clean-restore-task.html[Recover workloads with clean restore].
